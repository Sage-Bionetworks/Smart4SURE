Instructions:

Make sure your phone is charged.

Please complete your Activity Session as instructed. Each session contains the following activities:

  1. Rating your thinking, mood, pain level, sleep quality and level of exercise.
  2. Tapping on the phone screen repeatedly to test your motor speed and coordination. You will be asked to tap with your right and left hand.
  3. Saying “aaah” in the phone for as long as you can to record variations in your voice.
  4. Memory game where you are asked to recall highlighted objects.
  5. Holding your phone in several positions to measure tremor in your hands.
  6. Walking forward or standing still with your phone in your pocket to assess your gait and balance.
Prior to initiating a session make sure you are wearing pants with a pocket.
